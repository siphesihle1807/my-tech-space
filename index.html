Welcome to My Tech Space!

In My Tech Space, I’m excited to share the tools and resources that have been instrumental in my own tech journey. I’m passionate about offering you the same kind of support that helped me grow, whether you’re just starting out or already deep into development. From handpicked coding tutorials and detailed guides to the latest tech events, everything here is designed to help you succeed and enjoy your path in the tech world as much as I have.

Take a look around my site and discover:

  • Coding Resources: Dive into a curated collection of online platforms, tutorials, and courses that I’ve found invaluable for building and honing tech skills.
  • Tech Events: Keep up with exciting tech events and meetups both in South Africa and beyond, so you can connect with like-minded individuals and stay at the forefront of the industry.
  • Community Engagement: Become part of a vibrant community where you can share your journey, exchange ideas, and learn from others who share your passion for technology.

Maybe you're a newbie and trying to get your feet in tech, there's a million of us just like you. This isn't a pro starter pack into the field, but I know it's a good start.
