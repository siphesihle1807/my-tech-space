Tech Events in South Africa

South Africa hosts a variety of tech events throughout the year, catering to enthusiasts, professionals, and entrepreneurs. These events offer opportunities for networking, learning, and showcasing innovations.

Why Attend?

Attending these events helps you stay updated with the latest trends, meet like-minded professionals, and discover new career opportunities. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting, there's something for everyone.

Upcoming Events

Check platforms like Eventbrite and Meetup for the latest tech events near you. Here are some of the top events you might want to attend:

  • TECHSPO Cape Town: A tech event showcasing the latest innovations in technology, with international exhibitions in various cities.
  • Microsoft Events: Events designed for developers, IT professionals, partners, educators, and business professionals, featuring growth opportunities and learning experiences.
  • ETHCape Town: Connects tech leaders from across the continent to explore opportunities in FinTech, AI, blockchain, and more.
  • #buildwithayoba: Africa’s leading all-in-one super app, is transforming communication, entertainment, and e-commerce. You'll get to learn how you can partner with ayoba to elevate your projects, whether you're a developer, gamer, business owner, or content creator.
  • Neonomad: Join NeoNomad in Cape Town this First Thursday for their inaugural crypto workshop! You'll get to enjoy a beginner-friendly session on cryptocurrency, followed by a networking event. It’s a great chance to relax, meet new people, and explore the world of crypto.

Local Meetup Groups

To connect with tech enthusiasts in your area, consider joining a local meetup group. These groups can often host events, workshops, and social gatherings where you can learn and share knowledge.
